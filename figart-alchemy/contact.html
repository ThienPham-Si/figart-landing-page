Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide through our marketplace partners. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

How long does a custom order take?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and our current workload. We'll provide a specific timeline when discussing your project.

What materials do you use?

We primarily use high-quality PLA and resin for 3D printing, with metal components for certain pieces. All materials are safe and durable for long-term display.

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for retailers and specialty shops. Please contact us directly with your requirements for more information.
